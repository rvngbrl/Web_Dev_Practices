Arvin Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk (110째F/45째C)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
    • Brown Egg
    • White Egg
    • Quail Egg
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour

For the Filling

  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm milk and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Add the sugar, melted butter, salt, and eggs to the yeast mixture. Mix well.
  3. Gradually add the flour, one cup at a time, mixing until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  6. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C).
  7. Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface into a large rectangle (about 16x12 inches).
  8. Spread the softened butter over the rolled-out dough.
  9. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the buttered dough.
  10. Starting from the long edge, tightly roll up the dough into a log. Cut the log into 12 equal slices.
  11. Place the slices in a greased baking dish, leaving some space between each roll. Cover with a damp cloth and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
  12. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
  13. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before serving. Optionally, you can drizzle icing over the warm rolls.
